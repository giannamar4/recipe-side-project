Pancit

Pancit is a stir-fry dish typically made in a wok. My family uses bihon noodles, but you can use any type of rice noodle and choice of protein. i.e. shrimp, chicken or pork.

Ingredients
1 8.8 ounce package rice noodles
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
shredded chicken
1 yellow onion diced
6 cloves garlic minced
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 cup chicken stock
2 cups shredded cabbage
1/2 cup sliced carrots
3 diced green onions
3 lemons
  1. Soak noodles according to package directions.
  2. Prep the meat and vegetables by cutting into uniform sizes.
  3. Heat oil in a wok or large saute pan.
  4. Add pork, yellow onion, garlic and soy sauce. Cook 3-5 minutes or until pork is browned. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  5. Add chicken stock, cabbage, carrots and green onions. Cook 3-5 minutes or until vegetables have softened.
  6. Add noodles to pork and vegetable mixture. Toss to combine. Season to taste with salt,pepper and lemon.
